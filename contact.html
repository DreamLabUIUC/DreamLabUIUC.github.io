Join Our Team

Shape the future of AI for scientific discovery

We are actively seeking passionate and talented individuals to join our research group! Whether you're a prospective PhD student eager to push the boundaries of agentic AI, an undergraduate looking to gain hands-on research experience, a visiting scholar interested in collaborative projects, or someone seeking internship opportunities in cutting-edge machine learning research—we want to hear from you.

Our lab offers a dynamic environment where ambitious ideas meet rigorous execution. Work on moonshot projects that others might consider impossible, contribute to systems that are revolutionizing scientific discovery, and be part of a team that values both groundbreaking innovation and meticulous attention to detail. If you're excited about developing AI systems that can autonomously advance science, building tools that democratize complex analyses, or tackling the fundamental challenges in trustworthy machine learning, let's talk.

Ready to embark on an exciting research journey? Drop me an email with your CV, research interests, and what excites you about our work. I'd love to explore how we can work together to create transformative impact.
